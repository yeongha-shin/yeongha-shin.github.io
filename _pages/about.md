I am a `Ph.D Candidate` in Marine Robotics at `KAIST MORIN Lab`.

My research focuses on `autonomous navigation`, multi-agent collision avoidance, and human-vehicle interaction. I am particularly interested in developing intelligent maritime systems that enhance safety and decision-making in complex environments.

Before starting my graduate studies, I worked as a `navigator` at Hyundai Merchant Marine. This experience motivated me to transition into research on autonomous maritime systems.

Feel free to reach out for collaboration or research discussions.
